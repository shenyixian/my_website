About Me

About Me

Hi, my name is Liam Cheung! I grew up in Singapore and then I moved to Sydney, Australia.
I settled in Melbourne, a city known for its coffee and cultural references.
Before LBS, I studied Accounting and Finance at Melbourne University. I am a professional Viola player, and I was lucky enough to have survived the Christchurch earthquake in New Zealand back in 2011.
I was an apprentice at Adelaide Symphony Orchestra, where I was promoted to guest Violinist afterwards.
I co-founded the Go Society at University of Sydney and I have tutored many students to not only learn, but also enjoy playing Viola.

Professional Experience

I have interned at an asset management company where I covered SaaS and dairy companies. I then interned at PE and IB advisory firms covering Real Estate, Media and Industrials related transactions.

Why Am I Here:

Given the competitive nature of the industry, R and other data analytics platforms are vital for me to gain an extra edge in my future career in making sound investments.
